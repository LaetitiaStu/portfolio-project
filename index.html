I have often wondered why people wrote so many songs and poems about love. I enjoy them, don't get me wrong, and I often wish I had more capacity for the topic. But it is easiest after all to write about passion. And passion is as much about anger and frustration as it is about love and devotion. I think the one thing in this world that is easiest to be angry about is injustice. Therefore, much of what you will read is about passionate anger about injustice, and a sprinkle of my love for drama as well.
