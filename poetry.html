Poetry

Published poet and passionate bullshitter

I was told that the reason there are so many love poems in this world is, because it is easiest to write about emotion and passion. So, it seems only natural that most of my poetry has social or political roots. There is nothing more enraging than social injustice, and nothing more invigoratingly inspirational than the stories of people standing up for themselves, and the people around them.

Poetry Collection - I'll draw an Iris on my Heart and send it to You

"The book "I'll Draw an Iris on my Heart and send it to You" is a beautiful poetry book penned by author Helen J Storm. The book is mainly divided into three portions. The poetry contains around thirty poems penned on different topics of life be it emotional aspects of love & life, or its political characteristics. It is written in freestyle poetry to normal verses in a conventional form."
